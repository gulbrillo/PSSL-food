LunchPad

Lab meetings, lunch handled. Every good mission starts on a launch pad.

LunchPad is PSSL's home for lab-meeting food: one tap to RSVP, dietary needs counted automatically, caterers chosen by vote, and the reimbursement paperwork generated for you. Sign in with your PSSL account — nothing new to register.

What it does

✅

One-tap RSVP

"I'm in" or "Can't make it" — on your phone or laptop. Changed plans? Update any time; late changes are simply flagged.

🥗

Your diet, remembered

Set vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, allergies… once in your profile. Every RSVP counts them into the order automatically.

🍕

Picky-eater friendly

See the caterer for each meeting and attach a request — "please make sure there's a margarita pizza." It even remembers your usual.

❤️

Vote for caterers

An approved caterer list the lab actually likes: vote for favorites and shape where next week's lunch comes from.

🖨️

Paperwork, automated

Guests added in one click, and a printable sign-in sheet with signature lines for every meeting — reimbursement-ready.

🤖

Discord reminders

Link your Discord once and the lab bot nudges you before each RSVP deadline. You can even reply with /rsvp right in the server.

Why you'll use it

Ten seconds a week. No more "does anyone here eat gluten?" threads, no forgotten vegans, no missed lunches because you forgot to reply — the bot has your back.

Why the lab needs it

Whoever orders sees exact headcounts and dietary breakdowns per meeting, and the signed attendance sheet UF wants for food reimbursement prints itself.

Get started

lunchpad.darkcosmos.org

Sign in with your PSSL account → pick your food preferences once → tap "I'm in" this Thursday. That's the whole onboarding.
